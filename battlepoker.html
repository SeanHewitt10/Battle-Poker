Rules of the game

- Play poker hands to deal damage to the boss.

- Damage is calculated based on the value of the cards played and the poker hand. The better the poker hand played the more damage dealt.

- Discard or play cards from your hand to search for better cards from the deck.

- But be careful. You have a limited number of discards and hands to be played.

- Deal enough damage to bring the boss health to zero. Once the boss health reaches zero you can choose one power up before moving on to the next boss. Dont worry, your hands and discards will be reset and the deck will be reshuffled.

- Defeat all of the bosses to win the game! Good Luck!
